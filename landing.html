Frequently Asked Questions

What is PaperCraft?

‚ñº

PaperCraft is a professional paper packaging template generator that creates custom templates for cutting machines like Cricut, xTool, and Silhouette. We specialize in envelopes, boxes, bags, and other paper packaging solutions with precise cut lines and score lines.

How does the subscription work?

‚ñº

PaperCraft offers a simple $20/year subscription that gives you unlimited access to all template generators, custom sizing options, and premium features. You can cancel anytime, and your subscription will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Can I customize my templates?

‚ñº

Yes! You can customize dimensions, flap styles, corner types, and more. Our templates support custom sizing for any paper packaging need, with real-time preview and validation to ensure your templates will work perfectly with your cutting machine.

What file formats are supported?

‚ñº

We export templates in SVG format (optimized for cutting machines) and PDF format (for printing and reference). SVG files include separate cut lines and score lines, making it easy to use different tools for cutting and scoring.
